Mail-In Options

ClothingDonations.org is a NYC resource that allows residents to print their own shipping label to donate their clothes as a Mail-In alternative. All you have to do is type in your ZIP code, and press the Schedule Online button. This will bring you to a donation page where you fill out all the relevant information and you're able to print your label. There is a list of items on the page that are not being accepted. Accepted items include Clothing (Ladies, Men's, Children's), Shoes (anything but tennis shoes), Purses, Handbags and Wallets, Jewelry, Belts, and Accessories.
