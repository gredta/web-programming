Sustaine

Live Sustainably.

Fast fashion is one of the largest contributers of waste, pollution, and global warming. One of the best ways to reduce waste in our landfills is by recycling and donating our unwanted clothes. NYC has hundreds of drop-off locations and resources for residents to donate their unwanted articles (of clothing, shoes, accessories, and more!) and Sustaine's mission is to compile these resouces onto one easily-accessible site, in turn, reducing the amount of waste in our landfills and slowing down the consequences of global warming.

I added a marker in each borough for drop-off sites in NYC and will continue to research more drop-off location centers. I'm also planning on including mailing donation information for people who would rather ship their donations instead of going to their physical donation site.
