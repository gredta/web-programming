Sustaine

About Sustaine

Sustaine is a resource for people living in NYC to find donation drop-off locations and understand more about their local charities to have a better understanding of where their donations are going. The purpose of this website is to encourage more sustainable practices in the fashion industry as well as encourage charitability among New Yorkers, as finding donation drop-off locations and information about them can often be a struggle.

Upcoming Changes & Additions

I plan to add at least 50 more markers in NYC and I'm thinking about adding a section on the site that's a drop-off donation alternative for those who aren't able to visit a drop-off location and would rather ship off their donations to a charity of their choice. To do this I'm going to compile a list of charities that accept mail-in donations and compile donation pick-up resources for those who are unable to go to a drop-off location or the post office, which will be part of my research. I also plan on making this project a bit more interactive and collaborative, so if users know of a drop-off location that's not included on my map, I want to build a feature that allows users to add their own drop off location onto my map or submit their drop-off information to me and then I'll be able to add it to my map.

I included a preliminary wireframe of what the page would look like with the different donation options.

The user would decide what they would rather do, drop-off, mail-in, or request a pickup service and select the buttons on the bottom that would bring them to the next page with their desired resources.
